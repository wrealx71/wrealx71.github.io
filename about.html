ABOUT WREAL X

"I don't just make music. I bleed into the mic."

In the heart of East Africa, where the Nile begins and the red soil of Yei tells stories of resilience, a new voice emerged. That voice belongs to Angel Jambu Roy, known as Wreal X.

Born on July 20, 2007 in Yei Town, South Sudan, Wreal X is a Singer, Emo Rapper, and Songwriter.

🎵 The Sound

Wreal X moves between Emo Trap, Afrobeats, and Hip-Hop. He sings about the blues, romance, loss, and real life in South Sudan.

🌟 Inspirations

Juice WRLD, XXXTentacion, Lil Uzi Vert, Trippie Redd.
